rust_monopoly

Module rust_monopoly::board [−] [src]

Board keeps track of most of the game state local to the Monopoly board, including the spaces (and properties contained within them), the players, the card decks, and the background board image.

Most of the methods in Board do not take in a current player argument since this is already determined by the player_turn field in Board.
